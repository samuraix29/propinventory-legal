Privacy Policy

Inspectly · Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Inspectly is a professional property inventory inspection application designed for property clerks, inventory managers, and letting agents in the United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data transparently and responsibly.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@inspectly.app

2. What Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Account Data

When you create an account we collect your email address, display name, and sign-in method (Google, Apple, or email/password). If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive only the information those services choose to share — typically your email and name. We never see your Google or Apple password.

2.2 Subscription and Token Data

We collect your subscription plan and status, your token balance and transaction history, and the number of reports you have generated. We do not collect or store your payment card details — all payment processing is handled securely by Stripe, Inc.

2.3 Usage Data

We collect your last active date, total number of inspections and reports generated (count only), and your app version and device type for technical support purposes.

2.4 Inspection Content

Your inspection content belongs entirely to you. This includes property addresses, room descriptions, condition grades, photos, voice recordings, transcripts, and generated PDF reports. This content is stored on your device or your chosen cloud storage. We do not routinely access, read, analyse, or share the content of your inspections.

2.5 AI Processing

When you use the AI description generation feature, the room name, item name, condition grade, and optionally a photo of the item are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing. When you use voice transcription, your audio recording is sent to OpenAI Whisper. Neither service retains this data after processing. No personally identifiable information about you, your clients, or the property owner is included in these requests beyond what is necessary to generate the description.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the Inspectly service
  • To process your subscription and token purchases
  • To send important account notifications such as billing alerts and security notices
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve the app based on anonymised usage patterns
  • To prevent fraud and ensure account security
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data, use your inspection content for marketing, share your personal data with other users, or use your data for automated decision-making that affects you.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored on servers operated by Supabase, Inc., located in the European Union (Ireland). This means your data never leaves the EU and is fully subject to GDPR protections. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher and encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.

We never store passwords in plain text. Authentication is handled by industry-standard OAuth providers (Google, Apple) or Supabase Auth. Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.

If we become aware of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a UK or EU resident you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@inspectly.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Cookies and Tracking

The Inspectly mobile app does not use cookies. We do not track your behaviour across other websites or apps. We do not use advertising trackers or analytics that identify you personally.

10. Children's Privacy

Inspectly is a professional tool intended for use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make significant changes we will notify you by email and display a notice in the app. Your continued use of Inspectly after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
